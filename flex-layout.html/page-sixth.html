(IT)Information Technology

Information Technology (IT) encompasses the use of computer systems, software,
networks, and other digital technologies to manage, store, process,
and exchange information. It's a broad field that includes various aspects
like hardware, software, data management, cybersecurity, and
network administration. IT is crucial for businesses, governments, and
individuals, enabling efficient operations and facilitating access to information.
